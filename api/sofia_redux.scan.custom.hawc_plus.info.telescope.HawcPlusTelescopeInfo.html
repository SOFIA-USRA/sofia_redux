class sofia_redux.scan.custom.hawc_plus.info.telescope.HawcPlusTelescopeInfo[source]¶

Initialize the HAWC+ telescope information.

Contains information on the SOFIA specific telescope parameters such as zenith angle, boresight coordinates, and tracking status and the focus offset for HAWC+.
